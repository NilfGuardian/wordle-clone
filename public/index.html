WORDLE

Guess the word in 6 tries

How to Play

  • Guess the word: You have 6 chances to guess a random 5-letter word
  • Green: The letter is in the word and in the correct position
  • Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong position
  • Grey: The letter is not in the word
  • Submit: Press Enter after entering 5 letters
  • Delete: Use Backspace to delete the last letter
  • Score: Gain points based on attempts and time taken
  • Track Progress: View your stats and top scores on the dashboard
